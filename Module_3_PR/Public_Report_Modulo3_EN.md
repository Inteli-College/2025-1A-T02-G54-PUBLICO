# Nautic.Bot - Executive Summary

## Executive Summary

**Nautic.Bot** is a Brazilian nautical technology startup that develops portable rechargeable lithium battery aquatic turbines, remotely controlled via radio. The project represents a significant innovation in the aquatic equipment sector, offering accessible underwater mobility for water sports, recreational diving, nautical tourism, and maritime rescue operations.

The main highlights of the project include a wireless-controlled aquatic turbine, competitive prices compared to imported solutions, national technical support and recurring revenue model, modular design for easy maintenance, and a billion-dollar target market.

---

## 1. Product

The subnado aquatic turbine is already an existing and functional product. Remotely controlled system with ESP32-C3 Super Mini, long-range wireless communication, brushless motor, operation at adequate depths. Control via joysticks + buttons and safety system.
